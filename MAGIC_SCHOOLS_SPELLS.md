## 📊 Summary

**Total New School Spells: 200**
- 8 Schools × 25 Spells per School = 200 Unique Spells
- 5 Tiers per School × 5 Spells per Tier = 25 Spells
- Zero Overlap with Original 16-Spell Spellbook

**Key Mechanics:**
- **15% MinSB Reduction**: All school spells cost 15% less spellbook to cast
- **Dual Research Tracks**: `res_spellbook` and `school_spellbook` advance independently
- **One-Time Selection**: Choose school at `res_spellbook >= 100` (permanent choice)
- **Magic Race Bonus**: `school_spellbook` research gets magic race bonus
