# World Fragment Synergies

Activate a synergy by placing all 10 required fragments in the specified buildings. Only one synergy can be active at a time.

---

## 🔥⚒️ Infernal Crucible
*Chaos of creation and destruction; birth requires sacrifice*

**Passive — Forge Enhancement:** All weapons/armor crafted are +50% more potent in combat

**Active — Forge of Gods:** Instantly upgrade all troops to next rank
- Cooldown: 1 day
- Cost: 50% population, −50 stability (7-day penalty)

|---|---|
