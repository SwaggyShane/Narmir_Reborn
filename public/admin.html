✨ Prestige Tiers

Players can transcend their kingdom when reaching Level 50. Doing so resets their land, wealth, and population, but retains heroes, trade routes, and grants massive permanent scaling bonuses based on their Prestige Level.

Additionally, advancing in tiers increases the XP required for each level by 20%.

Tier Building Caps Economy (Markets/Trade) Combat Power Population Cap
Tier 1 +25% +5% — —
Tier 2 +50% +10% — —
Tier 3 +75% +15% +5% —
Tier 4 +100% +20% +5% —
Tier 5 +150% +30% +10% +25%

Note: Tier 2 unlocks race-specific special buildings and Tier 4 unlocks special race-specific troops (currently in development/design).
