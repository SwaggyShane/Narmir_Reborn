## Getting Started

### Account Creation

1. Click "Register" on the login page
2. Choose your kingdom name (unique across all players)
3. Select your race (affects starting bonuses)
4. Confirm your email address
5. Your kingdom is created with starting resources!

### Your First Turn

When you first log in, your kingdom has:
- **100 Gold** — Currency for trading and hiring
- **1,000 Wood** — Building material
- **500 Stone** — Fortification material
- **50 Iron** — Weapon material
- **0 Coal** — Energy resource (farm it through trade)
- **0 Steel** — Advanced weapons (craft from iron)
- **0 Mana** — Magic resource (research to unlock)

**Immediate Actions:**
1. Visit the **Building** panel → Build your first farm
2. Visit **Research** → Unlock basic technologies
3. Explore the **Market** → Buy resources you need

## Core Concepts

### The Kingdom

Your kingdom is your home base with:
- **Population** — Determines labor force and resource production
- **Happiness** — Affects production rates and loyalty
- **Defense** — Protects against attacks
- **Morale** — Affects troop performance in combat

### Turns

Time flows in **turns** (not real-time). Each turn represents several game hours.

- **Turn Duration:** ~15 minutes real-time (based on server load)
- **Automatic Turns:** Server processes turns periodically
- **Manual Turn Advancement:** Click "Next Turn" to advance immediately
- **Max 3 Turns/Hour:** Rate limiting prevents spam

### Progression

The game progresses through:
1. **Early Game** (Turns 1-50) — Build basics, learn game
2. **Mid Game** (Turns 50-200) — Expand, compete, specialize
3. **Late Game** (Turns 200+) — Warfare, alliances, domination

---

## Turn System

### What Happens Each Turn?

1. **Production** — Farms produce food, mines produce ore
2. **Consumption** — Population consumes resources
3. **Happiness Change** — Based on food and conditions
4. **Troop Training** — Barracks produce new soldiers
5. **Research Progress** — Labs advance research projects
6. **Market Updates** — Prices adjust based on supply/demand
7. **Event Resolution** — Expeditions complete, battles resolve

### Speeding Up Time

**Options to advance your turn:**
- **Click "Next Turn"** — Immediately advance (costs nothing)
- **Wait ~15 minutes** — Server auto-advances all players
- **Expeditions** — Can take multiple turns to complete

### Optimal Play Pace

- **Casual:** Check in once per day
- **Competitive:** Check in every 30 minutes
- **Hardcore:** Stay online constantly (turn/attack coordination)

## Resources

### The 9 Core Resources

| Resource | Source | Use | Storage |
|----------|--------|-----|---------|
| **Gold** | Trading, raids | Hiring troops, buying resources | ∞ (no limit) |
| **Wood** | Farms, forests | Building, shipbuilding | 50,000 max |
| **Stone** | Quarries, mines | Fortifications, buildings | 50,000 max |
| **Iron** | Deep mines | Weapons, armor, tools | 20,000 max |
| **Coal** | Trade only | Fuel, power | 10,000 max |
| **Steel** | Crafting (iron) | Advanced weapons | 5,000 max |
| **Mana** | Research only | Magic spells, enchantments | 2,000 max |
| **Food** | Farms, hunting | Feed population | 30,000 max |
| **Troops** | Barracks, mercenaries | Warfare, defense | ∞ (no limit) |

### Resource Production

**Wood:**
- Source: Lumber camps (1 per settlement)
- Rate: 50 wood/turn per farm
- Boost: Research "logging" for +10%

**Stone:**
- Source: Quarries (1 per settlement)
- Rate: 30 stone/turn per quarry
- Boost: Research "stoneworking" for +10%

**Iron:**
- Source: Deep mines (1 per settlement, requires mining research)
- Rate: 15 iron/turn per mine
- Boost: Research "metallurgy" for +15%

**Food:**
- Source: Farms (1 per settlement)
- Rate: 100 food/turn per farm
- Consumption: 10 food/turn per 100 population

**Gold:**
- Source: Trade, market arbitrage, raid rewards
- Rate: Varies (0-500 gold/turn depending on strategy)
- Uses: Hire mercenaries, buy rare resources

### Storage Limits

Each resource has a **maximum storage capacity**. Excess production is lost!

- **Tip:** Upgrade storage buildings to hold more
- **Tip:** Trade or use resources before hitting capacity
- **Tip:** Plan your production to match usage

## Warfare

### Combat System

Narmir's combat is **simultaneous and strategic**:

1. **Both armies attack at the same time**
2. **Damage is calculated per unit type**
3. **Combat is deterministic** (same input = same outcome)
4. **Casualties are permanent** (lost troops stay lost)

### Unit Types

| Unit | Cost | Strength | Weakness | Best Against |
|------|------|----------|----------|--------------|
| **Militia** | 5 gold | Low | None | Nothing special |
| **Swordsman** | 10 gold | Medium | Archers | Heavy units |
| **Archer** | 12 gold | Medium | Cavalry | Flying units |
| **Cavalry** | 20 gold | High | Spikes/Pikes | Archers |
| **Mage** | 50 gold | Variable | Silence spells | Mounted units |
| **Dragon** | 200 gold | Very High | Anti-magic | Everything |

### Attack Strategy

**Consider:**
- **Army Composition** — Mix unit types for synergy
- **Enemy Defenses** — Know what you're attacking
- **Resource Cost** — Is the raid worth the troops?
- **Retaliation Risk** — Can the enemy counter-attack?

**Example Attack:**
- Your army: 1,000 swordsmen + 500 archers
- Enemy has: 800 cavalry + 200 mages
- Cavalry weakness: Archers (good!)
- Mages weakness: ???
- **Result:** Losses on both sides, advantage to cavalry

### Defense

**Improve your defense by:**
1. **Building walls** — Reduce incoming damage by 20-40%
2. **Recruiting guards** — Stationed troops defend automatically
3. **Spells** — "Fortification" spell adds temporary protection
4. **Alliances** — Call allies for reinforcements

### Covert Operations

**Special attacks that avoid direct combat:**

| Operation | Cost | Effect | Detection |
|-----------|------|--------|-----------|
| **Espionage** | 100 gold | See enemy resources | Low |
| **Sabotage** | 500 gold | Damage buildings | Medium |
| **Assassination** | 1,000 gold | Weaken military | High |
| **Theft** | 250 gold | Steal resources | Low |

### Combat Log

Every attack is recorded in your **Combat Log**. Check it to:
- Review past battles
- Learn what works/doesn't work
- Understand why you lost/won
- Plan future attacks

## Exploration & Expeditions

### What Are Expeditions?

**Expeditions** send your troops to explore and gather resources from distant lands.

### Starting an Expedition

1. Open **Exploration** panel
2. Choose expedition type
3. Allocate troops
4. Click "Send Expedition"
5. Wait 1-5 turns for completion

### Expedition Types

| Type | Duration | Risks | Rewards |
|------|----------|-------|---------|
| **Treasure Hunt** | 2 turns | Low | Gold, gems |
| **Monster Hunt** | 3 turns | Medium | Equipment, XP |
| **Dungeon Delve** | 5 turns | High | Rare artifacts |
| **Trade Route** | 4 turns | Low | Resources |
| **Artifact Search** | 6 turns | Very High | Powerful items |

### Risk vs Reward

- **Safe expeditions** → Quick, guaranteed rewards
- **Dangerous expeditions** → Slow, high reward OR total loss
- **Balance:** Mix safe and risky for steady progression

### Expedition Management

**Active Expeditions View:**
- Shows all expeditions in progress
- Estimated completion time
- Risk level of each
- Ability to cancel (lose progress + some troops)

## Research & Upgrades

### Technology Tree

Your civilization advances through **research**. Available techs depend on:
- **Current tech level** (must research prerequisites first)
- **Happiness** (better happiness = faster research)
- **Lab buildings** (more labs = faster research)

### Research Examples

**Tier 1 (Early Game):**
- Farming (improve food production)
- Animal Husbandry (unlock cavalry)
- Basic Smithing (improve weapons)

**Tier 2 (Mid Game):**
- Metallurgy (increase iron production)
- Fortification (improve defense)
- Trade Routes (unlock market features)

**Tier 3 (Late Game):**
- Magic (unlock mana and spells)
- Shipbuilding (unlock naval combat)
- Alchemy (transmute resources)

### Research Points

- Each lab generates **1 research point/turn**
- Researching a tech costs **100-1000 research points** depending on tier
- Tip: Build multiple labs to accelerate research

## Economy & Trading

### The Market

Every player can **buy and sell resources** on the global market.

**How to Trade:**
1. Open **Market** panel
2. View current prices for each resource
3. **Buy:** Set price → Wait for seller to accept
4. **Sell:** Set price → Wait for buyer to accept
5. Transactions complete instantly

### Price Dynamics

Prices change based on:
- **Supply and demand** — Abundant wood = low price
- **Recent trades** — Spike in buying = price up
- **Seasonal events** — Festivals might boost certain resources
- **Player activity** — High-activity servers = more price movement

### Trading Strategy

**Profit from arbitrage:**
1. Buy wood when cheap (low demand, high supply)
2. Wait for price to rise (seasonal event, increased demand)
3. Sell at profit

**Tip:** Small profits compound! Buy 1,000 wood at -10%, sell at +5% = 1,500 profit per cycle.

### Trade Routes

**Unlock via research**, trade routes allow you to:
- Establish automatic trades with other kingdoms
- Set up recurring resource exchanges
- Lock in prices for long-term stability
- Earn a small commission as middleman

| Building | Cost | Produces | Bonus |
|----------|------|----------|-------|
| **Farm** | 500 wood | 100 food/turn | Population housing |
| **Lumber Camp** | 300 wood | 50 wood/turn | Can't be attacked |
| **Quarry** | 400 stone | 30 stone/turn | Fortifies nearby |
| **Mine** | 200 iron | 15 iron/turn | Requires mining tech |
| **Lab** | 600 stone | 1 research/turn | Affects all research |
| **Barracks** | 400 wood, 200 iron | Troops | Troop training center |
| **Wall** | 1000 stone | Defense +20% | Vital for defense |

### Building Strategy

**Early Game (Turns 1-50):**
- Build 3 farms (population)
- Build 2 lumber camps (wood production)
- Build 1 barracks (troop training)

**Mid Game (Turns 50-150):**
- Build specialized production (mines, labs)
- Build walls (defense)
- Build temples (happiness management)

**Late Game (Turns 150+):**
- Maximize production buildings
- Multiple barracks (army size)
- Multiple labs (research speed)
- Fortify with walls and towers

### Building Placement

**Consider:**
- **Adjacent to town center** — Resources flow faster
- **Defensive positions** — Place walls strategically
- **Cluster by type** — Efficiency bonuses for grouped buildings
- **Layout planning** — Save space for future expansion

## Heroes & Mercenaries

### Heroes

**Heroes are legendary warriors** that boost your army's performance.

**Recruit Heroes:**
1. Spend 1,000 gold in the Barracks
2. Random hero generated with random stats
3. Keep or reroll (costs more gold)

**Hero Stats:**
- **Strength** — Troop damage bonus
- **Defense** — Troop survival rate
- **Tactics** — Combat effectiveness bonus
- **Charisma** — Morale and recruitment bonus

**Hero Bonuses:**
- Each hero provides +5-15% to army performance
- Can have up to 3 active heroes
- Leveling heroes increases their bonuses

### Mercenaries

**Temporary troops** you can hire without training.

**Hire Mercenaries:**
1. Open **Mercenary Barracks**
2. Choose unit type and quantity
3. Pay upfront (2x normal cost)
4. Instant recruitment

**When to Use:**
- Rapid defense against incoming attack
- Quick army boost for important battle
- Filling gaps in your unit composition

**Mercenary Types:**
- Elite swordsmen (high damage)
- Archer corps (range advantage)
- Cavalry squadron (mobility)
- Magical regiment (special abilities)

## Rankings & Competition

### Ranking System

Players are ranked by:
1. **Kingdom Level** (experience from gameplay)
2. **Military Strength** (army size + quality)
3. **Economic Power** (resource wealth + production)
4. **Diplomatic Influence** (alliances, reputation)

### Leaderboard

**Top 100 players** displayed in global rankings.

**Rankings update:**
- Every 24 hours (or after major milestones)
- Based on cumulative score from all categories
- Competition resets seasonally (monthly)

### Seasonal Events

Special competitions every month:
- **Treasure Rush** — First to 1M gold
- **Monster Slayer** — Most monster kills
- **Trade Master** — Highest trading volume
- **War Games** — Most victories in combat

**Rewards:**
- Unique cosmetic items
- Resource bonuses for next season
- Prestige titles ("Treasure Master", etc.)

### Alliances

**Team up with other players!**

**Form an Alliance:**
1. Reach kingdom level 10+
2. Recruit 2+ members
3. Set alliance name and banner
4. Start collaborating

**Alliance Benefits:**
- Shared treasury (pool resources)
- Coordinated attacks
- Shared research (faster tech!)
- Group expeditions
- Alliance wars against other groups

## FAQ

### How do I increase happiness?

**Happiness comes from:**
- Building temples (+5 each)
- Having adequate food (+1 per 100 population with full storage)
- Winning battles (+2 per victory)
- Festivals (seasonal events, +20 temporary)

**To maintain high happiness:**
- Always have food in storage
- Win more than you lose militarily
- Build temples regularly

### What's the best unit type?

**There's no "best" — it depends on:**
- **Enemy composition** — Archers beat cavalry, cavalry beats archers
- **Your economy** — Expensive units (dragons) need high income
- **Map terrain** — Cavalry worse in forests, better on plains
- **Game stage** — Early game needs cheap units, late game can afford dragons

**General advice:** Mix unit types (40% swordsmen, 30% archers, 20% cavalry, 10% mages).

### How do I defend against raids?

1. **Build walls** — Reduce damage by 20-40%
2. **Recruit defenders** — Soldiers defend automatically
3. **Research fortification** — Unlock defensive techs
4. **Join an alliance** — Call reinforcements
5. **Use spells** — "Shield" spell adds temporary defense
6. **Play actively** — Being online means faster response

### What's the fastest way to get rich?

1. **Trade routes** — Set up with neighboring kingdoms
2. **Raid weak neighbors** — Steal their resources (risky!)
3. **Market arbitrage** — Buy low, sell high
4. **Complete expeditions** — High reward if successful
5. **Production focus** — Specialized economy (all farms = max food income)

### How do expeditions work if I'm offline?

**Expeditions continue even while you're offline!**

- Send expedition
- Log off
- Return 3 turns later
- Expedition complete, rewards in inventory
- Perfect for overnight play

### Can I trade with my friends?

**Yes! Two methods:**

1. **Market trades** — Buy from them at agreed price
2. **Trade routes** — Set up recurring automatic trades (post-unlock)

**To trade with a friend:**
1. Have them post resource on market at agreed price
2. Buy it immediately
3. Transaction complete!

### What happens if my kingdom is destroyed?

**Kingdoms don't permanently die** — you have a few options:

1. **Rebuild** — Start fresh, keep your name
2. **Join alliance** — Get help from members
3. **Restart** — Create new kingdom (old one becomes archive)

### How often should I log in?

**Depends on your play style:**

- **Casual:** Once per day (catch up on events, do some turns)
- **Regular:** Every 3-4 hours (stay competitive, active trading)
- **Hardcore:** Multiple times per hour (coordinated warfare, market watching)
